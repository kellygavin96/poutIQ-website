PoutIQ FAQ

Everything you need to know about discovering your perfect lip combo

What is PoutIQ? +

PoutIQ is an AI-powered app that identifies lip combos from photos or videos and suggests exact products or dupes. You can explore trending combos, save favorites, and share your looks with friends.

How much does PoutIQ cost? +

PoutIQ is free to download!

How does PoutIQ protect my privacy? +

PoutIQ processes all images and videos locally on your device when possible. We do not sell your personal data or usage information to third parties.

Is PoutIQ available for Android? +

Currently, PoutIQ is available on iOS. Android development is in progress — join our waitlist to get notified when it launches.

How do I report a bug or request a feature? +

You can submit feedback directly in the app or email us at support@poutiq.com. We review all suggestions to improve the app.

What devices and iOS versions does PoutIQ support? +

PoutIQ requires iOS 16.0 or later and works on iPhone and iPad. We optimize for all modern devices for the best AI analysis performance.

Can PoutIQ identify other makeup products? +

Currently, PoutIQ specializes in lip products and combos. Future updates may expand to foundation, eyeshadow, and other makeup items.
