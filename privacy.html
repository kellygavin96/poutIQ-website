PoutIQ Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we handle your data when using PoutIQ.

1. Information We Collect

When you use PoutIQ, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Account details such as email address or username when you sign up.
  • Content You Provide: Images or videos uploaded for AI analysis, saved lip combos, and preferences.
  • Usage Data: Interaction with the app, app performance data, and analytics to improve our services.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app version to optimize performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve AI lip combo analysis and app features.
  • Sync your favorites and preferences across devices.
  • Communicate with you about updates, promotions, or support requests.
  • Analyze trends and app performance to enhance user experience.

3. How We Protect Your Information

PoutIQ takes security seriously. Measures include:

  • Processing images locally on your device when possible.
  • Encrypting data sent to our servers for AI processing.
  • Using secure authentication and storing minimal personal information.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Use the app at your own risk.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only in limited circumstances, such as:

  • With service providers to support app functionality (e.g., cloud AI processing).
  • When required by law or to protect legal rights.
  • With your consent, or as part of aggregated, anonymized analytics.

5. User-Generated Content

Images, videos, and other content you upload may be used to improve the AI, display within the app, or for promotional purposes. You retain ownership of your content, but grant PoutIQ a limited license to use it for these purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

PoutIQ may use third-party services for analytics, cloud processing, or advertising. These services have their own privacy policies and are independent of PoutIQ.

7. Children's Privacy

PoutIQ is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such data, it will be deleted promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page, and continued use of PoutIQ constitutes acceptance of any changes.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@poutiq.com.
